⚠️ Risk & Mindset

Position sizing

Cut standard risk on a war-headline tape — half-size or less. Volatility widens stops; keep dollar risk constant by trading smaller, not by moving stops.

Narrative discipline

"War = oil up, stocks down" is priced. Your edge is the reaction to the next headline (sweep + MSS), not the headline itself. Don't marry the war thesis.

Upcoming catalysts

Any confirmed U.S./Iran strike overnight; Hormuz / Kharg Island blockade confirmation; further Fed-speak on the hawkish minutes; energy inventory data. Any one can gap the tape.

Overnight hold warning

Strikes explicitly threatened "tonight." Every overnight position is a gap bet. If you can't defend a gap against you, don't hold it.

ICT mindset reminder

Liquidity is engineered where emotion is highest — and fear is highest on war days. Let price take out the obvious stops first, wait for the CISD/MSS, then enter at a discount/premium (OTE). Patience beats prediction.
